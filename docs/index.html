Word Sifter

How to Use Word Sifter

  1. Enter your guess: Type your 5-letter word guess into the "Guess" field.
  2. Feedback: In the "Feedback" field, enter the color results from the game:
    • Use G for green (correct letter, correct position)
    • Use Y for yellow (correct letter, wrong position)
    • Use B for black or gray (letter not in the word)

    For example, if your guess was STEAM and the result was green, yellow, black, black, yellow, you would enter GYBBY.

  3. Update: Click "Update" or press Enter to see the filtered list of possible words based on your guess and feedback.
  4. Exclude used words: Select "Exclude used" to hide words that have been solutions in past games.
  5. Reset: Click "Reset" to clear your inputs and start over with the full word list.

Continue entering guesses and feedback to narrow down the possible words. Good luck!
